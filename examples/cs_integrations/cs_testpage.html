TU Wien

The TU Wien integration is a link-based integration (PSVS).
Users can click on links to either get a Knowledge Map or a Streamgraph for a predefined query on the OKMaps website.
Other parameters are default parameters.
  • BASE, Knowledge Map: sustainability
  • BASE, Streamgraph: sustainability
  • BASE, Knowledge Map: failpage
  • BASE, Streamgraph: failpage
